This temple is one of the most sacred sites for Hindus in India. Devotees flock this temple to take the blessings of Lord Shiva and also to perform the last rites of their loved ones. The Mahabaleshwara temple features beautiful Dravidian architecture and a 1,500-year old Shiva idol. All through the day, and especially the evenings, the temple resonates with activity and prayers and aartis dedicated to Shiva.
